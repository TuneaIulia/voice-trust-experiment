Instructions

Imagine that a company is evaluating voices for use in short online advertisements.

You will hear a series of promotional messages for different products, one at a time.

After each clip, please rate how trustworthy the speaker sounds in the promotional message, on a scale from 1 (not at all trustworthy) to 7 (extremely trustworthy).

Please focus on the speaker’s voice rather than on whether you personally like the product or agree with the advertising claim.

There are no right or wrong answers — please respond based on your honest first impression.
